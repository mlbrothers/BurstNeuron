Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the ExcuseMe app (hereby referred to as "Application") developed by Siddharth Patel (hereby referred to as "Developer"). The app is provided "AS IS" and is free to use.

Information Collection and Use

The Application uses your input prompts solely for excuse generation. These prompts are sent securely to OpenAI's API for processing. According to OpenAI's policy, they do not store or use your data for model training or any other purpose.

The Developer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All generated excuses are created on request and are not saved unless you choose to save them locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

This Application uses only the OpenAI API for excuse generation. It does not use ads, analytics, or other third-party SDKs.

Children

This Application is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Security

Your prompts are transmitted securely and discarded after processing. No input data is retained or reused.
