Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Drongo - Smart Learning Companion app (hereby referred to as "Application") developed by Siddharth Patel (hereby referred to as "Developer"). The app is provided "AS IS" and is free to use for educational purposes.

Information Collection and Use

The Application uses images and PDF documents you provide (via camera capture or file upload) solely for generating educational content including tutorials, quizzes, and flashcards. These documents are processed securely using OpenAI and Mistral API services. According to our service providers' policies, they do not store or use your data for model training or any other purpose beyond the immediate content generation request.

The Developer does not collect, store, or share any personal data, documents, or study materials. All learning content is generated on request and can be saved locally on your device at your discretion.

Document Processing

Your photos and PDF files are analyzed using advanced AI algorithms powered by OpenAI and Mistral to provide:

  • Comprehensive educational tutorials
  • Interactive quiz questions and answers
  • Personalized flashcard content
  • Adaptive learning recommendations

All processing occurs in real-time through secure OpenAI and Mistral API calls and no document data is retained after analysis.

The Developer assumes users have proper authorization for all uploaded content and is not responsible for copyright or intellectual property violations.

Third-Party Services

This Application uses OpenAI and Mistral API services for educational content generation and learning material creation. The app does not use ads, analytics, or other third-party tracking SDKs.

Local Storage

Any saved tutorials, quiz results, flashcards, progress data, or user preferences are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted or backed up to external servers.

Educational Purpose

This Application is designed specifically for educational and academic purposes. All generated content is intended to enhance learning and understanding of academic subjects.

Your documents and images are transmitted securely using encrypted connections to OpenAI's and Mistral's servers and are discarded immediately after processing. No document data is retained, cached, or reused.
