Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Roast My Friends: Funny Roasts app (hereby referred to as "Application") developed by Siddharth Patel (hereby referred to as "Developer"). The app is provided "AS IS" and is free to use.

Information Collection and Use

The Application uses images you provide (via camera or gallery) solely for generating humorous roasts and comedic content. These images are processed securely using OpenAI API services. According to our service providers' policies, they do not store or use your data for model training or any other purpose beyond the immediate roast generation request.

The Developer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All roast results are generated on request and can be saved locally on your device at your discretion.

Data Processing

Your photos are analyzed using advanced AI algorithms powered by OpenAI to provide:

  • Creative and humorous roast content
  • Multiple roast style options
  • Safe, entertaining commentary
  • Shareable comedic content

All processing occurs in real-time through secure OpenAI API calls and no image data is retained after analysis.

Third-Party Services

This Application uses OpenAI API services for roast generation and content creation. The app does not use ads, analytics, or other third-party tracking SDKs.

Local Storage

Any saved roasts, favorites, or user preferences are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted or backed up to external servers.

Content Guidelines

While the app generates humorous content, we strive to keep all roasts lighthearted and fun. The AI is programmed to avoid truly offensive, discriminatory, or harmful content while maintaining comedic value.

Children

This Application is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. Parental guidance is recommended for users under 18.

Security

Your images are transmitted securely using encrypted connections to OpenAI's servers and are discarded immediately after processing. No image data is retained, cached, or reused.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Continued use of the app implies agreement with the current policy. Users will be notified of significant changes through app updates.
